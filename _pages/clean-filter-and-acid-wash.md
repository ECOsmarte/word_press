<center>
<h2>The filter needs to be cleaned and acid washed:</h2>
</center>
<ol>
<li>Shut off the pump and close the valves.</li>
<li>Take off the valve head. </li>
<li>Unscrew the drain plug.</li>
<li>Drain the water until the media is visible (this may take a while).</li>
<li>Inspect the surface of the media for clumps of debris and remove.</li>
<li>Pour in 2 gallons of liquid acid, completely covering the media. Let the filter sit overnight for 12 hours.</li>
<li>Backwash the filter to "waste".</li>
</ol>
