<h3>Are the stains blue green?  Has anyone added chlorine or a chlorinated product to the ECOsmarte water?  If chlorine was added to an ECOsmarte pool it will combine with the copper and produce copper chloride, which adheres to the pool surface.  To remove the stains the pool has to be drained and washed with hydrogen peroxide:</h3>

<ol>
<li>Drain the pool.</li>
<li>Apply hydrogen peroxide with a garden sprayer.</li>
<li>Let it bubble for about 10 minutes.</li>
<li>Rinse it off and repeat the process one more time.</li>

</ol>
