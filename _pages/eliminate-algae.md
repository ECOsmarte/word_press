<center>
<h2>To eliminate algae:</h2>
</center>
<ol>
<li>Begin lowering the pH to 6.8 - 7.2. (Aim for 6.8). For tips to Lower pH levels, <a href ="http://ecosmartecloud.com/lower-ph" target="_blank">Click Here</a></li>
<li>Apply 1 quart non-metal algaecide during daylight hours.  If possible, apply the algaecide in the morning, which is when algae feeds.</li>
<li>Wait 12 hours, then put in non-chlorine shock (potassium monopersulfate), using the formula 5 lbs per 20,000 gallons of pool water. </li>
<li>Wait 24 hours.  If the pool isn't all the way clear, add another dose of the non-chlorine shock.</li>
</ol>
