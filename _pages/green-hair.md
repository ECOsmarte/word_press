<center>
<h3>In blonde hair, chlorine-based hair products will react with the copper, causing a green tinge.  Rinsing the hair in lemon juice will remove the green tinge.

If chlorine-based products are not being used and there is still green hair, thoroughly rinse hair before swimming.

Unfortunately, some hair will still get a green tinge even if it is rinsed before swimming. If this happens, simply remove the green tinge with lemon juice.
</h3>
